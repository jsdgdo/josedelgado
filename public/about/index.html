The fun is in figuring it out

Always gravitating toward design since the begining of my career in software, starting as a developer, the web gave me the outlook and the tools to express myself in a visual and interactive way. Armed with semantic HTML, Javascript, and most of all CSS, my journey in design started to take shape.

First in the browser and then into Sketch & Figma, the way I learned to design let me onto further levels of abstraction but always keeping in mind the needs of my fellow engineers.

Hitting the grownd running I got to be a part of an agency, then helped building an online presence for 3 of the most read dailies in the country, after that I was designing expriences for an exciting startup. I’m eating it all up and learning every day.

As oppossed to traditional learning structures (two-time college dropout here) I find that the best way to learn for me is through exprience and lots of reading and research, my intuition and experience fill the gaps successfully.

Work samples and case studies are coming soon, for now if you want to know more about my work shoot me an email! I’d love to have a chat.
